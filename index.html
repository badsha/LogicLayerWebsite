Our process

Simple steps to
extraordinary results

01
Discovery & Scoping

We invest time upfront to deeply understand your goals, constraints, and tech landscape. No boilerplate proposals — you get a precise scope, timeline, and cost estimate tailored to your reality.

02
Team Assembly

We handpick engineers from our vetted roster whose skills, timezone, and working style match your project. No random CVs — every person on your team has been chosen with intention.

03
Agile Development

Two-week sprints, daily standups, transparent Jira/Linear boards. You see progress every day, not just at the end. Full access, full visibility, zero surprises.

04
Launch & Scale

We don't disappear at launch. We stay on through your initial growth, optimize under real load, and transition knowledge cleanly to your internal team when you're ready.
